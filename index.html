Privacy Policy for HCIN Connect

Effective Date: November 17, 2024

At HCIN Connect, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using our app, available for download on the Google Play Store. By using the HCIN Connect app, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection

HCIN Connect collects minimal user data to enhance the functionality of the app. The app collects the following types of data:

Call Session Information: We collect the start and end times of your call sessions to monitor the session duration. Language Preferences: We collect the language requested during the call to ensure a smooth communication experience. We do not collect any other personal or sensitive information such as government IDs, payment details, or financial information.

2. Data Use

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:
To provide and enhance the HCIN Connect app's functionality, including accurate session tracking.
To ensure the language preference during the call is honored.
We do not use your data for any other purpose, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Sharing

HCIN Connect does not share any personal or sensitive user data. We do not disclose, sell, or share your call session details or personal preferences with any third parties, except as required by law or for the purposes of compliance with applicable legal obligations.

4. Data Security

HCIN Connect is committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data. We use encryption to protect your data during the entire call signaling and communication session. This helps secure your data from unauthorized access.
