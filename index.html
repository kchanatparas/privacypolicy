This privacy policy disclose how the HCIN connect app collects, uses, and shares user data. The HCIN connect app does not publicly disclose sensitive user data like government IDs, payment information, or other financial information. The HCIN connect app limit access, collection, use, and sharing of personal and sensitive user data. The HCIN connect app follows Cisco WebEx and Google's Families policy. The HCIN connect app will not share user data, it only collects the start and end time of a call session, and also the language requested in the call. The HCIN connect app does not collect users data and therefore users do not need to request data deletion when uninstalling the app. The HCIN connect app uses data encryption during the entire call signaling and communication session.
